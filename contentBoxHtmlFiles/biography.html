Biography

Where did i learn to code

I started working with computers around 15 when i built my very first computer. Ever since then ive had an extreme passion for all things tech. A few years later i got my A+ and began to learn about managing large server databases. I quickly found that i enjoyed the coding aspect of tech even more than the management side of it. I spent around 2-3 years learning as much code by myself as i could and then took a class on full stack development.
